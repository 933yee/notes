# Events and Probability

#### Experiment
A process to produce an outcome

#### Random Experiment
Experiment whose outcome is not known until the process is done

ex1: 翻一個硬幣兩次
ex2: 翻硬幣直到 head 出現

#### Sample Space
The set of all outcomes of an experiment

ex1: {HH, HT, TH, TT}
